# Addressing employee burnout: Are you solving the right problem?

Employers have invested unprecedented resources in employee mental
health and well-being. With burnout at all-time highs, leaders wonder if
they can make a difference. Our research suggests they can.

This article is a collaborative effort by Jacqueline Brassey, Erica Coe, Martin Dewhurst, Kana Enomoto,
Renata Giarola, Brad Herbig, and Barbara Jeffery, representing the views of the McKinsey Health Institute.
