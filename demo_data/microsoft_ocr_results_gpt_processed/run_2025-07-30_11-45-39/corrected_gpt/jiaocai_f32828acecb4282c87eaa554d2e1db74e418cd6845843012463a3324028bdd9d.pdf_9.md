# Local First

Is a non-profit organization that leads the
development of an economy grounded
in local ownership that meets the basic
needs of people, builds local wealth
and social capital, functions in
harmony with our ecosystem, and
encourages joyful community.

Local isn't a place, it is people. It's reintegrating
our lives, connecting in practical ways with the
people we live and work with. Local First is
another way of saying "People first."

# Why Local First?

1\. Local First Engages. It creates and deepens
relationships within a community, which becomes more
vibrant through collaboration and mutual investment.

2\.
Local First Humanizes. Physical and economic
development occur on a scale that nurtures people and
encourages beauty in natural and manmade
environments.

3\.
Local First Preserves. When more citizens are
genuine stakeholders, protecting local economic and
environmental value is of mutual interest.

4\.
Local First Strengthens. When citizens and local
businesses work together, it catalyzes positive
development and the community has structural integrity
to withstand economic downturns.

5\.
Local First Invests. Sustainable local economies and
ecosystems grow into markets that are greater than the
sum of their parts.

6\.
Local First Encourages. Intimate connections
between customers and businesses facilitate innovation,
interaction, and discovery, making for a more vibrant
and resilient local economy.

7\.
Local First Enriches, Reinvesting success in
customers, employees, and the local environment makes
the entire community wealthier.
