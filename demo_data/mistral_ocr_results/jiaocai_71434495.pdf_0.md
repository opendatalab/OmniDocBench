1. 求定积分 $\int_{0}^{5} \frac{\sin x \cos x}{\sin ^{3} x+\cos ^{4} x-5} \mathrm{~d} x$.
[答案]: 解令 $t=\sin ^{2} x$, 则 $\mathrm{d} t=2 \sin x \cos x \mathrm{~d} x, \sin ^{4} x+\cos ^{4} x=2 t^{2}-2 t+1$, 则

$$
\text { 原式 }=\frac{1}{2} \int_{0}^{\frac{1}{2}} \frac{1}{2 t^{2}-2 t-4} \mathrm{~d} t=\frac{1}{12} \int_{0}^{\frac{1}{2}}\left(\frac{1}{t-2}-\frac{1}{t+1}\right) \mathrm{d} t=-\frac{\ln 2}{12}
$$

[答案]: 解不妨记 $D_{1}=\{(x, y) \mid 0 \leqslant y \leqslant x \leqslant \sqrt{\pi}\}$, 由对称性可知

$$

$$
