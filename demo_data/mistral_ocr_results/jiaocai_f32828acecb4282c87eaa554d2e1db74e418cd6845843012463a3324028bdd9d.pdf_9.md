## Why Local First?

1. Local First Engages. It creates and deepens relationships within a community, which becomes more vibrant through collaboration and mutual investment.
2. Local First Flammables. Physical and economic development occur on a scale that nurtures people and encourages beauty in natural and mammals environments.
3. Local First Preserves. When more citizens are genuine stakeholders, protecting local economic and environmental value is of mutual interest.
4. Local First Strengthens. When citizens and local businesses work together, it captures positive development and the community has structural integrity to withstand economic downtimes.
5. Local First Invests. Sustainable local economies and ecosystems grow into markets that are greater than the sum of their parts.
6. Local First Encourages. Intimate connections between customers and businesses facilitate innovation, interaction, and discovery, making for a more vibrant and resilient local economy.
7. Local First Enriches. Entertaining success in customers, employees, and the local environment makes the entire community wealthier.
