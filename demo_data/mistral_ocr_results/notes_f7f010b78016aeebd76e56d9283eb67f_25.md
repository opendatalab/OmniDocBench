61. who. what

Who 指约多或多来，what 指取 12 或他位.

62. what. which

What 的英㑂基地爱与限制的。which 至一交管国内进行英㑂。
which do you prefer, bananas or apples?
63. other. another

64. not a little. not a bit.
not a little 非零，not a bit - 点也不

65. many. much. a lot of
many 余可数名词查用。much 余方可数名词查用，a lot of 可数。
