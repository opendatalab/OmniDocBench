25- 015 Mixed Cations and Structural Complexity in $\left(\mathbf{E u}_{1-x} \mathbf{C a}_{x}\right)_{4} \mathbf{I n}_{3} \mathbf{G e}_{4}$ and $\left(\mathrm{Eu}_{1-x} \mathrm{Ca}_{x}\right)_{3} \mathrm{In}_{2} \mathrm{Ge}_{3}$ - The First Two Members of the Homologous Series $\mathbf{A}_{\mathbf{2}(\mathrm{n}+\mathrm{m})} \mathbf{I n}_{\mathbf{2 n}+\mathrm{m}} \mathbf{G e}_{\mathbf{2}(\mathrm{n}+\mathrm{m}]}(\mathbf{n}, \mathbf{m}=\mathbf{1}, \mathbf{2}, \cdots \infty ; \mathbf{A}: \mathbf{C a}, \mathbf{S r}, \mathbf{B a}, \mathbf{E u}$, or Yb). $\mathrm{Eu}_{1.23} \mathrm{Ca}_{2.77} \mathrm{In}_{3} \mathrm{Ge}_{4}$ (I), $\mathrm{Eu}_{2.60} \mathrm{Ca}_{1.40} \mathrm{In}_{3} \mathrm{Ge}_{4}$ (II), $\mathrm{Eu}_{0.30} \mathrm{Ca}_{2.70} \mathrm{In}_{2} \mathrm{Ge}_{3}$ (III), and $\mathrm{Eu}_{0.66} \mathrm{Ca}_{2.34} \mathrm{In}_{2} \mathrm{Ge}_{3}$ (IV) are synthesized from the elements in a molten In metal flux $\left(960^{\circ} \mathrm{C}, 20 \mathrm{~h}\right)$. The compounds are characterized by single crystal XRD, magnetic susceptibility measurements, and TB-LMTO band structure calculations. (I) and (II) crystallize in the space group $\mathrm{C} 2 / \mathrm{m}$ with $\mathrm{Z}=2$, and (III) and (IV) in the space group Pnma with $\mathrm{Z}=4$. The polyanionic substructures of the compounds are based on $\mathrm{InGe}_{4}$ edge--sharing tetrahedra, $\mathrm{Ge}_{2}$ dimers, and bridging In atoms in a nearly square planar environment. - (YOU, T.-S.; TOBASH, P.
